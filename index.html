Welcome to C-Cube Lab

We take human-centered approach to ignite innovation!

C-Cube lab is a human-computer interaction research group in the Graduate School of Communication and Arts at Yonsei University. In our lab, we are taking a human-centered approach to conduct interdisciplinary research that solves problems in various domains including technology, design, and social science. Our major research interest is to apply computational skills and methods to the process of making and distributing information. Stay tuned for our research updates!

Computing is fundamentally a social activity. Innovative thinking across many disciplines is required to deal with such environment.
